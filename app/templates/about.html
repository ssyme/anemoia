Why Anemoia?

The news-aggregate and rss-reader market is extremely saturated, so why a new one. Earlier this year I was looking for a news reader but despite the humungous range out there, I was dissapointed. I just wanted a simple and minimalist news reader. I didn't need any complex news-room-like features and I didn't want an overcomplicated and out-dated ui. That's why I built anemoia. A platform designed to solve these problems, with a simple, intuitive and ultra-minimalist modern ui.

What's next for Anemoia

Currently, anemoia is in an MVP(minimum viable product) stage. Development is ongoing! If you would like to keep up to date, or even contribute, the entire project is open-sourced via a github repository There are many planned features coming soon, here is a brief list - for a more detailed plan visit the repo.

  • * User accounts: Users will be able to log into the platform to save their settings, their favourite news sources and sources they'd rather ignore.
  • * Anemoia Premium: Don't worry - this is not a subscription model! Users would be able to upgrade for a one-off fee of around $7. This would support the platform and ensure development continues, it would also remove (unintrusive - these are already implemented) ads from the platform.
